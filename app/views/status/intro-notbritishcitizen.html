3. If you're not a British citizen

If you’re 16 or over and a British national abroad but not a British citizen, you need to:

  1. Check if you’re eligible with your nearest British embassy, high commission or consulate.
  2. Download and fill in an application form.
  3. Paper application forms should be scanned and emailed to your nearest British embassy, high commission or consulate, along with any supporting documents and photos, otherwise an appointment will be required to submit the application, supporting documents and photos.
  4. The relevant British embassy, high commission or consulate will contact you to take payment when the application is submitted by email.
