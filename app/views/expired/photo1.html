About your photo

You will need to add a recent photo for your emergency travel document. This is what a good photo looks like.

Tips for a good photo

  • Use a plain background
  • Face the camera with your eyes open and visible
  • Don't smile
  • Keep your mouth closed
  • Take your glasses off
  • Keep your hair away from your face
  • Don't wear a hat or any headgear (unless for religious reasons)
  • Make sure there are no shadows on your face or behind you
  • The photo should include your head and shoulders and have space around it
  • You don't have to crop your photo, as we will do it for you

Help taking a photo

If you are on a desktop or laptop computer and don't already have a photo saved, you may find it easier to add a photo using a device that takes photos, such as a mobile phone or a tablet.

I have a printed photo

If you cannot take a digital photo you can send a physical photo however this will delay your application. Photos can be sent to: British Consulate Malaga Calle Mauricio Moro Pareto, 2 Edificio Eurocom 29006 Malaga Spain.

I can't add a photo

You can add a photo at any time over the next 5 days. We will not be able to process your application until a photo has been added.

You could consider asking someone to help you take your photo such as a holiday rep or hotel staff. Many photo shops will also offer a digital photo service.
