Add your travel tickets

If you have printed tickets, you can take a photo of them and add them here. Make sure details like your name, destination and dates are included in the picture.

If you have emailed tickets, you may find it easier to take a screenshot .

I can't add a file

You can add a file at any time over the next 5 days. We will not be able to process your application until this file has been added.

You could consider asking someone to help you photograph your documents, such as a holiday rep or hotel staff. .

If you cannot add files, you can send physical documents, however this will delay your application. Documents can be sent to: British Consulate Malaga Calle Mauricio Moro Pareto, 2 Edificio Eurocom 29006 Malaga Spain
