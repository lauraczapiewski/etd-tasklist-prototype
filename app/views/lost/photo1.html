About your photo

You will need to supply a photo for your emergency travel document. This is what a good photo looks like.

Tips for a good photo

  • Use a plain background
  • Face the camera
  • Don't smile
  • Keep your mouth closed
  • Take your glasses off
  • Keep your hair away from your face
  • Make sure there are no shadows on your face or behind you
  • You don't have to crop your photo, as we can do it for you

If you need more detail, you can read the indepth photo guide and/or watch the photo guide video (optional)
