Take your photo

If you are using a phone or tablet that can take photos, you can take a photo and upload it now.

If you have an existing photo, you can upload it now. It must be less than 4 months old and meet the photo requirements. Your photo may be in your pictures, photos, downloads or desktop folder. Or in an app like iPhoto.

We’ll store all photos for up to 30 days in line with our privacy policy(Opens in a new window).
